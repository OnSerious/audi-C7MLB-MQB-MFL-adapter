IC: CKS CKS32F103CBT6 LQFP-48. Clone of STM32F103CBT6
VR: HT7533-1

LIN connected to USARTs through resistors and 1AM5 transistors (level shifting?)



Pinout
	white connector on the left pointing down, left to right:
		1 - 3V3
		2 - SWDIO
		3 - SWCLK
		4 - GND


Blue LIN master to steering wheel. USART3
	RX from wheel is PB11
	TX to wheel is PB10

Orange LIN slave to steering wheel electronics. USART2
	RX from car is PA3
	TX to car is PA2
